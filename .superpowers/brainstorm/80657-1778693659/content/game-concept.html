A — "Does this feel off?" alignment game

Show them a UI with one subtle misalignment, wrong spacing, or off-colour. They have to spot it. Tests visual sensitivity. Designers with a trained eye will get it instantly; others will stare. Reveals how much their eye has been tuned.

your taste, mapped

B — Rapid design instinct quiz

5–6 fast "pick one" choices: two layouts, two colour palettes, two typographic styles. No right answer. At the end: "You're a ______ designer" — a short, resonant personality type based on their picks. The mirror moment.

C — Spot the UX crimes

Show a deliberately bad UI screen (wrong CTA colour, bad hierarchy, confusing label). Tap to flag the problems. 3 issues hidden in it. More of a puzzle — satisfying to "win." Feels like a design critique exercise.

D — Kerning challenge

Drag a letter left/right to find the "perfect" kerning for a pair. Your answer is compared to the typographically correct spacing. Classic designer nerd test — simple, tactile, and surprisingly hard to get right by feel alone.
