4 archetypes — scored by 2 axes
The Surgeon

Cuts everything that doesn't belong. Precision over decoration. Your work is clean because you know exactly what to remove.

Rich + Structured
Builds systems that hold. Every detail earns its place. Your designs feel considered because they are.
