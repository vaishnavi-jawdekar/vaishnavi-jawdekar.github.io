A — Tap to pop quote, auto-dismiss

Tap lifts the book and shows your quote above it. Disappears after ~2s or on next tap. Same feel as desktop hover, just intentional.

B — Tap to flip (toggle)

First tap flips, second tap flips back. Stays flipped until you dismiss. Discoverable and fun — works identically on desktop and mobile.
